Kupo, "express passion to vision," operates the traditional stage lighting gears from more than twenty years ago to today specialized innovated illumination manufacturer for venues throughtout Asia, Europe, America, Africa and Oceania.

Kupo supports a full range of vastly entertainment lighting fixtures (and custom-made products) with highest quality standards, high reliability, fasten delivery and professional services around the worldwide in the lighting market.
